Important: This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your Fitbit data for research purposes. Please read it carefully before authorizing access to your data.

1. Data We Collect

When you authorize our research application, we may collect the following types of data from your Fitbit account:

  • Activity Data: Steps taken, distance traveled, calories burned, active minutes, floors climbed
  • Sleep Data: Sleep duration, sleep stages (light, deep, REM), sleep efficiency, time in bed
  • Heart Rate Data: Resting heart rate, heart rate zones, heart rate variability
  • Profile Information: Age, gender, height, weight (if you've provided this to Fitbit)
  • Device Information: Device type, battery level, last sync time

2. How We Use Your Data

Your Fitbit data will be used exclusively for research purposes, including:

  • Analyzing health and fitness patterns across populations
  • Understanding sleep quality and its relationship to daily activities
  • Studying the effectiveness of health interventions
  • Publishing research findings in scientific journals (data will be anonymized)
  • Improving public health recommendations and guidelines

3. Data Security and Storage

We take your data security seriously:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • Access Control: Only authorized research personnel have access to the data
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored on secure, password-protected servers
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and updates
  • De-identification: Personal identifiers are removed or encrypted when possible

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We will NOT share your personal data with third parties except:

  • With your explicit written consent
  • When required by law or legal process
  • In aggregated, anonymized form for research publications
  • With research collaborators who have signed data use agreements

We will NEVER sell your data or use it for commercial purposes.

5. Data Retention

Your data will be retained according to our research protocol:

  • Active Phase: Data collected during the study period (specify duration)
  • Analysis Phase: Data retained for analysis and follow-up studies (typically 3-5 years)
  • Long-term Storage: Anonymized data may be retained for future research
  • Deletion: You can request data deletion at any time (see Your Rights section)

6. Your Rights and Choices

As a research participant, you have the right to:

  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke authorization at any time through your Fitbit account
  • Access Your Data: Request a copy of the data we've collected about you
  • Data Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Data Deletion: Request deletion of your data from our systems
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data during international transfers, including:

  • Adequacy decisions by relevant privacy authorities
  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Binding corporate rules

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our authorization website may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Ensure proper functioning of the authorization process
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website usage (anonymized)

We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking purposes.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. If we make significant changes, we will:

  • Post the updated policy on this website
  • Notify participants via email (if contact information is available)
  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
